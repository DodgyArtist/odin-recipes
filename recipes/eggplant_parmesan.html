Melanzane alla Parmigiana

The Klank version!

Ingredients

Eggplant Stuffing

  • 1-2 large eggplant
  • 1-2 cloves garlic
  • 1 bunch of parsley, chopped
  • 1 egg
  • 1/4-1/2 cup of fine bread crumbs
  • 1/4 cup of parmesan cheese (Plus 1/4 cup more before baking)
  • 1 bunch of basil chopped into strips
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1 tablespoon tomato paste

Eggplant topping

  • 1/4 cup parmesan cheese
  • dried bread crumbs to taste
  • Olive Oil
  • Fresh Mozzarella Medallions
  • Fresh basil strips for garnish

Preparation

  1. Bring enough water to submerge the eggplant to a boil.
  2. Use a fork to puncture the eggplant just like when baking a potato.
  3. Blanch the eggplant(s).
  4. Place a sheet of parchment paper onto a cookie sheet.
  5. Set the eggplants aside to cool.
  6. Chop 1-2 cloves of garlic.
  7. Chop the bunch of parsley.
  8. Julienne the fresh basil (chop into strips).
  9. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees.
  10. Once the eggplants are cool enough to touch, cut them into quarters.
  11. Peel the skin and set aside.
  12. Put the pulp into a large bowl and chop.
  13. Combine with chopped garlic, parsley, egg, 1/4 cup of breadcrumbs (add more if not binding), Parmesan, Chopped basil, tomato paste, salt and pepper.
  14. Spoon lumps of the mixture onto the eggplant skins and lay them on the cookie pan.
  15. Drizzle with olive oil.
  16. Sprinkle with dried bread crumbs to taste.
  17. Cook for 20 minutes until a light crust forms.
  18. Remove tray and place medallions or slices of fresh Mozzarella over the eggplant.
  19. Return to oven and allow the cheese to melt. Our preference is for the cheese to only melt rather than burn.
  20. Remove from oven and let rest for 5 minutes.
  21. Sprinkle with remaining basil. Serve and enjoy!
